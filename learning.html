Computer Parts

Quiz

Click on any part on the left hand side to learn about it!

CPU - Central Processing Unit

This is like my Monster's brains. It allows them to make decisions and decide what to do... With this a computer can make decisions.

RAM - Random Access Memory

I can't have my creations forgetting everything all the time. RAM is like their short-term memory. Computers need RAM to think about decisions.

MBD - Motherboard

The motherboard is like the skeleton of my creation. In a computer, it allows everything else to communicate and work together.

PSU - Power Supply Unit

Like my creations need energy from food, the computer needs electricity as its food, which it gets from the PSU.

HDD - Hard Drive Disk

This is like the long-term memory of my monsters. I don't want them to forget who I am. A computer uses this to store information you want to save for later.

GPU - Graphics Processing Unit

Commonly called a Video Card, the video card makes things look good on your screen. Like my creation's eyes, the better they are, the better they see.
